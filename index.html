Visit Eindhoven

City of Light

The largest city in the south of the Netherlands, Eindhoven is bursting with innovation, design, creativity and technology.

Culture

During the annual, innovative light art festival, GLOW, Eindhoven's landmarks are illuminated.

Nature

The city has numerous parks and several nature reserves in the surrounding area.

Nightlife
